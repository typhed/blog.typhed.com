You are reading a freemium post. The title, summary, and tags above stay public,
so a first-time visitor and a search engine both get the gist. The body unlocks
when you sign in with a free TyPhed account, the same login that works on the
main site.

## Why gate anything at all

Most of the blog is fully public. A handful of posts ask you to sign in first,
and the trade is plain: you get the full write-up, and we get a way to reach you
when the next one lands. No payment, no catch.

## The content still renders normally

Once it unlocks, a members post is an ordinary MDX post. Math typesets:

$$
\sum_{k=1}^{n} k = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

Code highlights:

```ts
export function isSignedIn(session: Session | null): boolean {
  return session !== null
}
```

Same pipeline, same theme. The only difference is the soft gate sitting in front
of this body until you sign in.
